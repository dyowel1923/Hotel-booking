System User Roles

Role 1: Government Admin
Provincial Tourism Department
  • Manage & approve establishment registrations
  • View all bookings system-wide
  • Generate tourism reports & analytics
  • Manage tourist spots & destinations
  • Set system-wide policies & fees
  • Manage user accounts (all roles)
  • Publish announcements & events
  • Monitor payments & revenue
Super Admin
Role 2: Establishment Admin
Hotels, Resorts, Restaurants, Venues
  • Register establishment (pending approval)
  • Manage rooms, services & pricing
  • View & confirm booking requests
  • Manage availability calendar
  • Communicate with tourists (inbox)
  • Process check-ins & check-outs
  • View revenue & booking reports
  • Respond to reviews
Establishment Manager
Role 3: Tourist
Domestic & International Visitors
  • Register & manage profile
  • Search & browse establishments
  • Book rooms, services, events
  • View/cancel bookings
  • Make online payments
  • Receive e-confirmations via email
  • Leave reviews & ratings
  • Save favorites / wishlist

Feature Modules

Auth Module
Multi-role login & registration, email verification, password reset, remember token, middleware guards per role, session management.
Booking Engine
Real-time availability check, conflict detection, booking reference generation (PLW-YYYY-NNNNN), multi-step form, service add-ons, status workflow.
Payment Gateway
PayMongo / GCash integration, payment status webhook, receipt generation, refund workflow, tourism fee auto-calculation & remittance.
QR Check-in
Auto-generate QR code per booking, email QR to tourist, establishment scans QR to check in, status auto-updates to 'checked_in'.
Review System
Post-stay review gating (only completed bookings), 1–5 star rating, establishment reply, admin moderation, avg_rating cache update.
Reports & Analytics
Tourism arrivals dashboard, revenue by municipality, top establishments, booking trends (month/year), Excel & PDF export via Laravel Excel + DomPDF.
